Beyond general information, you should learn about how to solve problems that concern the following: 1. Matching logical symbols to their corresponding operations (negation, conjunction, disjunction, conditional, biconditional). 2. Applying de Morgan's Laws and the contradiction theorem to transform statements. 3. Determining whether compound statements are logically equivalent or identifying tautologies. |
